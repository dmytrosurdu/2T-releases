\section*{Motivation and idea}\label{sec:uom-summary-001}
Conformal Cyclic Cosmology (CCC) explains the low-entropy start of each aeon by a conformal handover from the previous aeon. This note proposes a \emph{finite-step, operational realization} of that handover: a quantum measurement-like bridge that \emph{creates classical records} and flips an $\AdS_4$ receiver brane into $\dS_4$ aeon brane after finitely many ``ticks'', \emph{without introducing additional free parameters.} 

The starting stance is simple: \emph{Unitarity cannot produce facts.} A purely unitary, invertible channel cannot produce certifiable facts on infinite-time tail. Operationally, the bridge state (AdS side) is \emph{pure}, \emph{factless}, high-energy vacuum.

This pure state $\AdS_4$ brane is built layer by layer from the previous aeon $\dS_4$ brane via pointer-aligned QND (POVM-compatible) channel on the radiative sector and almost-QND gravitational coupling (which produces two-point Diósi-Penrose self-energy scaling) to the canonical commutant KMS bath (hence no spurious heating in $\dS_4$/CFT sector). A Brown–York/anomaly DC rectifier then biases the receiver curvature, driving the $\AdS_4$ brane region to a split that detaches the next $\dS_4$ aeon in a finite number of pulses, “ticks”, that have discharged information to the $\AdS_4$ receiver.
